Gokarna

Gokarna is a coastal town known for its pristine beaches and religious significance. It offers a blend of spirituality and natural beauty, with attractions like Om Beach, Kudle Beach, and Mahabaleshwar Temple.

Badami

This historical town is famous for its rock-cut cave temples dating back to the 6th century. Badami showcases intricate carvings, sandstone cliffs, and ancient architecture, making it a significant archaeological site.

Chikmagalur

Nestled in the Western Ghats, Chikmagalur is a picturesque hill station renowned for its coffee estates, scenic landscapes, and trekking trails. It's an ideal destination for adventure enthusiasts and nature admirers.
